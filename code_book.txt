
Descriptive fields
=================

subject: ranges from 1-30; used to identify each of the 30 participants in the study

activity_name: identifies 1 of 6 activities performed:
		WALKING 
		LAYING
		WALKING_UPSTAIRS
		WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
		SITTING
		STANDING
		LAYING

=================

tBodyGyroJerkMag
fBodyAcc-XYZ
fBodyAccJerk-XYZ
fBodyGyro-XYZ
fBodyAccMag
fBodyAccJerkMag
fBodyGyroMag
